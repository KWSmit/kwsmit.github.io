Robots & more by Kees Smit

Welcome to my website about LEGO Mindstorms robots and more. Through this website I like to share my experiences and thoughts about building robots and programming.

I'm not a professional programmer, just an enthousiastic hobbyist. All of the information on this site is the result of my personal learning process: experimenting, reading books, searching Internet, forum posts, etc. I can't guarantee all information is 100% correct or well enough explained. So be careful when using anything of it. Don't take anything for granted. I just hope I can inspire you or can give you a hint or idea for your own projects.

I try to direct you as much as possible to the places I find very helpful. So you can find your own way in looking for further information.

You are free to use all my programs and robot designs. All programs on this site and in my Github repositories worked fine for me, but I can't guarantee they will work for you. For sure my programs can be improved in syntax and style. Any suggestions about this are welcome.

Also have a look at my YouTube channel KSmEV3.
